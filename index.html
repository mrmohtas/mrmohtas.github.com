  2. About

    Who Am I?

    I'm a twenty something designer and developer from the coastal town of Margate, Kent. I spent most of my childhood covered in sand or splashing about in the sea, I was often found making elborate sandcastles that took all day to build or drawing chalk masterpieces on the promanade. That was the early signs that I was going to grow up to do something in the creative field.

    I took a bit of a left turn during my teenage years and rebelled against the educational system so hard I fell out, spending most of my late teens and early twenties drinking and being a bit of a party animal. Once I grew out of that phase I got back to what I've always loved, making beautiful things and seeing my imagination come to life (be it digital or in a physical medium).

    I've now been working professional for over 6 years and continually trying to learn and do more, freelancing for the majority of the time but enjoying the few times I've been within a team environment.
