Who Am I?

I have been working as a designer and a developer for over 8 years, with experience in projects that have utilised many different disciplines. These include brand identity and website design to creating websites with my knowledge of front-end, and back-end development. I spend my time creating engaging user interfaces and mobile friendly apps.

I've been making sites since I was a youngster and the Internet was announced with the sounds of buzzes and beeps. My first site would be nearly 16 years ago now, using FrontPage and it was a joy to create and learn this shiny new technology.

Many years have past since that first attempt and I'm furthering my knowledge on a daily basis as the Internet evolves at amazing speed. The majority of my work has been spent freelancing for international clients and companies. Recently I've taken full-time roles within agencies and I enjoy working within a close knit team environment.
